The Best Movies According to Tirasha

My top three movies all-time.

The Pursuit of Happyness

Will Smith as Chris Gardner, a struggling salesman and single father who pursues a career in stockbrokerage. Based on a true story, the movie shows his hardships and triumphs in San Francisco.

The Karate Kid

12-year-old Dre Parker (Jaden Smith) could've been the most popular kid in Detroit, but his mother's (Taraji P. Henson) latest career move has landed him in China.

3 Idiots

Two friends are searching for their long lost companion. They revisit their college days and recall the memories of their friend who inspired them to think differently, even as the rest of the world called them "idiots".
